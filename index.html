Hi, I am Kang,
I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Prof. Carlos A. Meriles’ group at the City University of New York (CUNY),
specializing in quantum sensing with diamond color centers.

Prior to joining CUNY, I completed a two-year postdoctoral fellowship in Prof. Qihua Xiong's group at Tsinghua University,
where my research primarily focused on the opto-electronic and optical properties of 2D semiconducting materials.
I obtained my Ph.D. from Nanjing University under the guidance of Prof. Feng Miao,
where my doctoral research centered on the strain-engineered electrical transport properties of 2D materials and devices.

This multidisciplinary journey across several fields has endowed me with deep scientific insights and strong experimental skills.
Moving forward, I am eager to continuously push my academic boundaries and uncover exciting, high-impact questions in science and technology.
