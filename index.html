About

Experimental physicist with a builder's mindset.

My work sits at the intersection of quantum control, scientific instrumentation, and materials physics. I build and optimize measurement platforms that connect optics, microwave/RF control, timing electronics, data acquisition, and software automation.

I use this site as a compact professional profile and as a future home for notes on quantum sensing, lab automation, instrument control, and lessons from building research systems.
