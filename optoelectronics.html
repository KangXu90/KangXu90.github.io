Optoelectronics

Build photodetector with hands

Two-dimensional semiconductors provide a versatile platform for light-matter interaction, exciton physics, and device engineering. My postdoctoral work at Tsinghua focused on using layered crystals and heterostructures to build high-performance optoelectronic devices.
