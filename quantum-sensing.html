Quantum Sensing

We like defects in diamond

We use shallow color centers in diamond as local spin probes for nanoscale sensing. The central motivation is to bring quantum measurement close to chemically and physically active interfaces, where water, ions, oils, charge carriers, and engineered nanostructures behave differently from the bulk.
